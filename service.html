Cinematic shots for events, real estate, and media production. Capture perspectives that ground cameras can't reach.

Precision Agriculture

Boost yields with multispectral crop health maps, pest detection, and variable‑rate application planning.

  • NDVI & thermal analysis

Infrastructure Inspection

Safe, fast inspections of bridges, towers, pipelines, and construction sites with high‑resolution cameras and LiDAR.

  • Real‑time video feed

Environmental Monitoring

  • Change detection
  • Long‑term data sets
