We offer DGCA-certified drone pilot training programs for various skill levels. Our certifications are recognized nationwide.

Courses range from 5-day intensives to 6-week comprehensive programs, with flexible schedules.

No prior experience needed. We train from the ground up, including safety and regulations.

We provide all equipment. Advanced students may bring their own if preferred.
