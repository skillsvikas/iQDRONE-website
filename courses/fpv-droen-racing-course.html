FPV Racing & Freestyle

5 Days Immersive – Build, Fly, Race

Course Modules

  • FPV Drone Build: Frame, motors, ESCs, flight controller, FPV camera & VTX assembly
  • Simulator Training: LiftOff, DRL, Velocidrone – muscle memory & crash recovery
  • Betaflight Tuning: PID tuning, rates, filters, OSD configuration
  • Basic Maneuvers: Hover, turns, split‑S, power loop, matty flip
  • Freestyle Tricks: Inverted flight, wall taps, proximity flying
  • Racing Techniques: Gate practice, race lines, throttle control, race starts
  • Immersive Cinematography: CineWhoop flying, smooth freestyle shots
  • Field Repair & Maintenance: Soldering, motor replacement, antenna repair
  • Battery & Electronics Safety: LiPo care, fire prevention, charging best practices
  • Final Race & Freestyle Challenge: Timed race + judged freestyle run
  • Total Duration: 5 Days (intensive)
  • Classroom & Build: 2 days
  • Flight Training (field): 3 days
  • Mode: Offline (simulator + outdoor flying)
  • Timing: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • Batch Size: 6–8 students (maximum)
  • Age: 18 years and above
  • Prerequisite: Basic drone flying experience recommended
  • Physical: Good hand‑eye coordination, no medical restrictions
  • Equipment: All FPV gear provided during course
  • Course Fee: ₹35,000 + GST
  • Includes: Simulator access, FPV drone build kit (to keep), goggles & radio for training
  • Certification Fee: Included
  • Group Discounts: 10% for 3+ participants
  • EMI Options: Available on request
  • Government ID Proof (Aadhaar/Passport/Driving License)
  • Passport Size Photographs (2 copies)
  • Medical Fitness Certificate
  • Registration Form (provided by iQdrone)
  • Payment Receipt
