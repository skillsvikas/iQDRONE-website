Course Modules

  • Multispectral Sensors: NDVI, NDRE, thermal indices, sensor calibration
  • Crop Health Analysis: Stress detection, nutrient deficiencies, early pest warning
  • Precision Agriculture: Variable rate prescription maps, zone management
  • Variable Rate Spraying: Integration with spray drones, flow control, real‑time adjustments
  • Irrigation & Soil Moisture: Thermal imaging for water stress, drainage planning
  • Data Processing: Pix4Dfields, DroneDeploy, Agras mapping software
  • Reporting: Generating actionable farm reports, sharing with agronomists
  • Yield Prediction: Using historical data and AI models for forecasting
  • Integration with Farm Machinery: Connecting drone data to tractors, sprayers
  • Practical Evaluation: Field project with real crop analysis & prescription map
