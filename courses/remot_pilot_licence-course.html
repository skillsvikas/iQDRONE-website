Course Syllabus

  • Drone Flight Rules & DGCA Regulations
  • Drone Aerodynamics (Fixed-wing, Rotorcraft, Hybrid)
  • Navigation, Communication & ATC Procedures
  • Weather and Meteorology for Drones
  • Simulator Training: emergency procedures, disorientation, circuits
  • Basic Maintenance & Assembly
  • Practical Flying: take-off, hovering, turns, figure-8, night flying

Day‑wise Breakdown

  • Day 1: Registration, Stakeholders & laws, Basic Flight Principles, ATC, Fixed-wing/Rotorcraft/Hybrid aerodynamics
  • Day 2: Q&A, Weather, Equipment Maintenance, Risk Assessment, Payload, Intro to Drone Data
  • Day 3: Theory Test, Simulator Training (3 hours), Basic Maintenance & Assembly (2 hours)
  • Day 4-5: Practical Flying (Digital Sky, basic maneuvers, circuits, figure-8, auto mission, night flying, final test)
  • Total Duration: 5 Days (intensive)
  • Ground Training: 2 days

  • Practical Training: 2 days
