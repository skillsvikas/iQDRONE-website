Typical Agenda (3‑Day Workshop)

Day 1 – Build & Understand

  • Introduction to drone parts & tools
  • Step‑by‑step assembly
  • Battery handling & safety
  • Flight controller basics
  • Simulator familiarisation

Day 2 – Fly & Control

  • Pre‑flight checks & take‑off
  • Basic manoeuvres (hover, turns)
  • Disorientation recovery
  • Circuit flying & patterns
  • Outdoor flight practice

Day 3 – Advanced & Explore

  • Introduction to FPV
  • Aerial photography basics
  • Troubleshooting & repairs
  • Mission planning
  • Certificate distribution
